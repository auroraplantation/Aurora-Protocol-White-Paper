# Aurora Points Program

The Aurora Points Program is a growth incentive system designed to reward early participants and build a committed community ahead of the $AUR token launch. Points function as **airdrop weight** — they determine each participant's proportional allocation in the future $AUR token generation event.

---

## Program Overview

Aurora Points are non-transferable, non-tradeable units earned by participants through four primary channels. Points accumulate over time and are tracked off-chain by Aurora Labs. At the $AUR token generation event, each participant's share of the total $AUR airdrop will be proportional to their share of total points earned across all participants.

**Points are not tokens.** They have no monetary value, cannot be redeemed for cash or stablecoins, and do not represent ownership or equity in Aurora Protocol or Aurora Digital Labs Limited.

---

## Earning Channels

| Channel | Weight | Description |
|---|---|---|
| **Purchase** | 40% | Points earned when purchasing RWA tokens in a primary sale |
| **Holding** | 25% | Points earned for holding RWA tokens over time (duration-weighted) |
| **Direct Referral** | 25% | Points earned when a referred user purchases RWA tokens |
| **Indirect Referral** | 10% | Points earned from purchases by users referred by your direct referrals |

### Channel Details

**Purchase (40%):**
Points are awarded at the time of RWA token purchase, proportional to the USDC amount invested. Larger purchases earn more points. This channel rewards capital commitment.

**Holding (25%):**
Points accrue continuously while a participant holds RWA tokens. The longer you hold, the more points you earn. This channel rewards long-term commitment and discourages speculative flipping.

**Direct Referral (25%):**
When a participant refers a new user and that user makes their first RWA token purchase, the referrer earns points based on the new user's purchase amount. Each participant receives a unique referral code.

**Indirect Referral (10%):**
If your direct referral (User B) refers another user (User C), and User C purchases RWA tokens, you (User A) earn indirect referral points. This creates a two-layer incentive for network growth.

## Referral Structure

### Two-Layer Cap

The referral system is strictly limited to **two layers of depth**:

```
You (Layer 0) ──► Direct Referral (Layer 1) ──► Indirect Referral (Layer 2)
                                                          │
                                                    ✗ No Layer 3+
```

There is no third layer or deeper. This is not a multi-level marketing structure. The two-layer cap prevents the creation of extractive referral pyramids.

### Referral Cap

Total referral earnings (direct + indirect combined) are capped at **35%** of a participant's total points. This ensures that no participant can earn a majority of their points solely from referrals — meaningful capital participation is required.

## Tier System

Participants are classified into four tiers based on cumulative points and activity. Higher tiers receive enhanced points multipliers.

| Tier | Name | Multiplier | Requirements |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Seed** | 1.0× | Default tier for all participants |
| 2 | **Sprout** | 1.2× | Minimum points threshold + active holding |
| 3 | **Growth** | 1.5× | Higher points threshold + referral activity |
| 4 | **Harvest** | 2.0× | Top-tier points threshold + sustained participation |

Tier multipliers apply to all future points earned. They do not retroactively adjust previously earned points.

## Growth Enhancements

Beyond the four core earning channels, the Points Program includes additional engagement mechanisms:

### Referral Milestones
Bonus points awarded when a participant reaches referral count thresholds (e.g., 5, 10, 25, 50 referrals).

### Squad Challenges
Group-based challenges where a team of participants collectively earns bonus points for meeting aggregate targets (e.g., total squad investment volume).

### Quality Multiplier
A bonus multiplier applied when referred users demonstrate sustained engagement (holding tokens beyond a minimum period, participating in multiple batches). This rewards quality referrals over quantity.

### Social Quests
Points earned for verified community engagement activities such as content creation, educational contributions, and community support.

## Compliance

The Aurora Points Program is designed to comply with **Hong Kong's Companies Ordinance (Cap. 617)** and relevant securities regulations:

- Points are explicitly **not securities, tokens, or financial instruments**
- Points have **no cash value** and are **non-transferable**
- The program does not constitute an offer or sale of securities
- Referral structures are capped at two layers with percentage limits to avoid characteristics of prohibited schemes
- All program terms and limitations are disclosed to participants before enrollment

## $AUR Airdrop Conversion

At the future $AUR token generation event:

1. A snapshot of all accumulated points will be taken
2. Each participant's percentage of total points will be calculated
3. The designated $AUR airdrop allocation will be distributed proportionally

**Example:**
- Total points across all participants: 10,000,000
- Your points: 50,000
- Your share: 0.5%
- If $AUR airdrop pool is 1,000,000 AUR → you receive 5,000 AUR

The exact $AUR airdrop pool size, vesting schedule, and conversion timeline will be announced separately and are not defined at this time.
