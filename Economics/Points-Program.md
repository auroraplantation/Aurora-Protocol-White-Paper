# Points Program

> **Aurora Protocol's Points Program rewards active participation across the ecosystem, with accumulated points serving as the weighting basis for future `$AUR` governance token airdrops.**

---

## Overview

The Aurora Points Program is designed to incentivize meaningful engagement — not just speculation. Points are earned through actions that directly contribute to protocol health: investing in batches, holding tokens, and growing the community through referrals.

Points are non-transferable, non-tradable, and carry no monetary value. Their sole utility is to determine each participant's proportional weight in future `$AUR` token distribution events.

---

## Earning Channels

Aurora Points are earned through **4 primary channels**:

| Channel | Weight | Description |
|---------|-------:|-------------|
| **Purchase** | 40% | Points earned proportional to USDC invested in primary sales |
| **Holding** | 25% | Points accrued over time for holding RWA tokens without selling |
| **Direct Referral** | 25% | Points earned when a referred user makes their first investment |
| **Indirect Referral** | 10% | Points earned from investments made by second-degree referrals |

> **Total referral weight is capped at 35%** of any individual's total points to prevent gaming.

---

## Referral Structure

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Maximum Referral Depth** | 2 layers (direct + indirect) |
| **Direct Referral Reward** | 25% weight of referred user's purchase points |
| **Indirect Referral Reward** | 10% weight of second-degree user's purchase points |
| **Combined Referral Cap** | 35% of total individual points |

The 2-layer maximum and 35% cap are designed to encourage genuine community growth while preventing multi-level referral schemes.

---

## Tier System

Participants are classified into **4 tiers** based on cumulative points:

| Tier | Name | Multiplier | Qualification |
|------|------|:----------:|---------------|
| 1 | **Seed** | 1.0× | Entry level — all participants |
| 2 | **Sprout** | 1.5× | Sustained engagement threshold |
| 3 | **Growth** | 2.0× | Significant investment and referral activity |
| 4 | **Harvest** | 3.0× | Top-tier contributors |

Higher tiers earn a points multiplier applied to all future earning channels, rewarding long-term and consistent participation.

---

## Growth Enhancements

Beyond the core 4 channels, the Points Program includes additional engagement mechanics:

| Enhancement | Description |
|-------------|-------------|
| **Referral Milestones** | Bonus points awarded at referral count thresholds (e.g., 5, 10, 25 referrals) |
| **Squad Challenges** | Group-based earning events where teams compete for bonus allocations |
| **Quality Multiplier** | Additional points weight for referring users who actively invest (not dormant accounts) |
| **Social Quests** | Points earned for completing specific community tasks (e.g., content creation, governance participation) |

---

## $AUR Airdrop Mechanics

Points serve as the **weighting basis** for `$AUR` governance token airdrops:

| Parameter | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Airdrop Calculation** | User's share = (User Points / Total Points) × Airdrop Pool |
| **Token Type** | `$AUR` — separate governance token (not RWA tokens) |
| **Token Status** | Not yet issued — planned as a future protocol milestone |
| **Snapshot** | Points balances will be snapshotted at announced dates prior to distribution |

> *`$AUR` is a planned governance token. Its issuance timeline, total supply, and distribution mechanics will be announced separately. Points accumulation does not guarantee any specific token allocation or financial return.*

---

## Compliance

The Points Program is designed to comply with Hong Kong's Securities and Futures Ordinance (Cap. 617):

| Compliance Measure | Description |
|--------------------|-------------|
| **Non-Transferable** | Points cannot be traded, sold, or transferred between accounts |
| **No Monetary Value** | Points carry no intrinsic financial value and are not redeemable for cash |
| **No Yield Promise** | The program makes no guarantees about `$AUR` token value or distribution timing |
| **Anti-Gaming** | Referral caps and depth limits prevent multi-level marketing structures |
